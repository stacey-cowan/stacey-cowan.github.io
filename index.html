Let Me Introduce Myself

My name's Stacey! And I do creative design things!

Why I Design

Design is fulfilling:
Getting each pixel in the perfect place;
Choosing the right colors and fonts;
Making it all come together;
And seeing the look on someone's face when you give them exactly what they wanted and made it even better.
It's about making things look great, but also function great. Making sure people notice it, remember it, think about it.
Designers are complicated beings.
